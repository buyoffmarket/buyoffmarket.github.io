How It Works

Search for a property

Tell us where you want to buy a house, the minimum number of bathrooms, bedrooms and your maximum budget.

Get matched with home owners

We’ll match you with home owners who have not put their property on the market but would be willing to sell.

Show your interest

We’ll reach out to the home owner and let them know you are interested in their property.

Make the deal

If they like your offer, we’ll connect you and you take it from there. We can put you in touch with local partnered Real Estate professionals, legal services and mortgage providers for any support you need.
