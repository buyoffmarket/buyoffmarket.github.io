What Is Off Market Real Estate?

Off Market real estate, or off market listing is when a property is sold outside of MLS/Realtor and/or through a real estate agent.

When searching properties through our site, we can provide insights to home-owners who may not have considered selling their property, but are now inclined to do so with the offers on the table. This gives the buyer a huge advantage as they are the first to express interest in the property.
